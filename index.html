DENARIUS
161-180 AD SILVER

A denarius was a high value coin that would be worth about a days worth of labor.

This coin was made of silver and minted in Rome.

FRONT

"Marcus Aurelius"

BACK

"Harmony of the emperor, holder of tribunician power for the 16th time, consul for the third time."
